This page is all about me, Alec

I grew up in North Texas interested in design, art, and performance from a young age. My undergraduate degree is in Theatrical Design and Production from Oklahoma City University in 2015.

In my spare time, I like to keep busy. I might be 80 feet up climbing granite in the Wichita Mountains. Or - at home tinkering in the garage on woodworking projects, throwing clay on my pottery wheel, or learning how to fix something in my historic home. It could be a Dungeons and Dragons night, board games day, or an afternoon with a new video game. I might be traveling - flying out to Florida, driving to El Paso, down in Dallas, or spending the week in Nicaragua. But most nights you'll find me cooking up something delish for dinner.

Now-and-days I live in Oklahoma City with my wife Tiffany, daughter Gwen, two dogs Bilbo and Frodo Baggins, and our cat Lyra.
