Hello, I'm Alec (he/him).
I'm a digital product designer living and working remotely in Oklahoma City.

My passion is rooted in creating products and experiences that impact others positively. Currently, I design at Prenda where I've built the Prenda Design System, created a marketplace for learners to connect with teachers, and reimagined aspects of the brand and brand story.

Prior to this I spent three and a half years at LegalShield growing the design team from a group of two to a group of ten. While there I lead the product design group, worked to redesign LegalShield mobile experience, and helped to reimagine the LegalShield brand.

I made this website to talk about my work and to provide a place where I can write & share my thoughts around design.
